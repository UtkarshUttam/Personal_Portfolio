About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Utkarsh Uttam, and I am a B.Tech student at Vellore Institute of Technology, Bhopal. My passion for programming and technology started at a young age, and I have been exploring different languages and tools ever since. I have experience in HTML, CSS (including Bootstrap), PHP, JavaScript, Python, C/C++, and MySQL, as well as developing desktop applications using Python in KAVACH OS. Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have completed several projects that have allowed me to apply my skills and creativity. These include a landing page for defence knowledge, a simple banking GUI using Python's tkinter library, a note-sharing platform for VIT students, a responsive hostel website for managing hostel activities, and a desktop application for hackathons and internships using the QT framework of Python. I am a quick learner, a problem-solver, and a team player. I enjoy collaborating with others to achieve common goals and create innovative solutions. In my free time, I like to explore new technologies, read tech-related blogs, and contribute to open-source projects. If you are interested in working together on a project or learning more about my skills and experience, please feel free to contact me. Thank you for visiting my portfolio website, and I hope you enjoy browsing my projects!
